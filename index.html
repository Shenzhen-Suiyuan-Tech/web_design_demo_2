Single Egg

Crafted from nothing, shaped by light and patience

A quiet study in form, material, and motion

Then another follows, shaped slowly by the same patient hand
Rows begin forming on their own, with no script to follow
Each shell catches the light differently, yet none compete for it
Texture keeps repeating across every surface, never twice the same
A shared warmth moves between them, carried shade by shade
Patience that scales without ever once feeling rushed
What was singular slowly becomes a quiet, gathered multitude
Still fragile in every piece, still exact, still entirely whole
No two surfaces catch the light in exactly the same way
The count keeps growing, yet the care behind it never thins

Multiplying across every row without losing the original standard of care
Every single row repeats the same exacting standard, never once a shortcut
Nothing here is truly mass-produced, only carefully produced again and again
The many only exist because the very first one was never treated carelessly

Countless Care
